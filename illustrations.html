I’ve been drawing digitally since 2019. During that time, I’ve managed to develop my own style, which I would describe as colorful and cartoony. My two biggest achievements as an illustrator are “MINI Planets” (a series of drawings used to decorate Munich’s MINI Pavillion) and “Hoodie” (a full length webcomic). To view more of my art, visit my instagram page @nesha_0230.
