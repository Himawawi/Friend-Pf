- DER BABA -




Brand design (2022)


Project type: Real-life client work

Der Baba is a middle eastern restaurant located in the heart of Munich. In order to make it stand out from its competitors, a few steps were taken:

One: The logo/mascot was created to resemble a friendly, middle-eastern man
Two: A series of short animations were made to highlight the restaurants unique traits
Three: To tie it all together, a group of posters was created to decorate the restaurant’s interior

Since middle eastern food is often hot & spicy, red was chosen as the main color of this updated brand identity.

Software used: AfterEffects, Illustrator
